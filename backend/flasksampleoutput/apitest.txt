Endpoints to test the Flask endpoints with

Health check: GET http://localhost:5001/api/health
Get news: GET http://localhost:5001/api/news?days=7&limit=5
Predict custom events: POST http://localhost:5001/api/predict
News + Predict (market): GET http://localhost:5001/api/predict-from-news
Market prediction: GET http://localhost:5001/api/market/predict-from-news
Personal prediction: GET http://localhost:5001/api/personal/predict-from-news
Sample market data: GET http://localhost:5001/api/sample-data?data_source=market
Sample personal data: GET http://localhost:5001/api/sample-data?data_source=personal
